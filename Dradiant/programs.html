At D'Radiant School, we offer a range of comprehensive programs tailored to nurture your child's unique potential at every stage of their development.

Toddler Program (2.5-3.5yrs)

A gentle introduction to structured learning through play, sensory activities, and socialization.

Key Focus Areas:
  • Sensory development and exploration
  • Basic communication and language skills
  • Fine and gross motor skills development
  • Social interaction and emotional development
  • Building confidence and independence

Preschool Program (3.5-4.5yrs)

Building foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and creative expression within a play-based curriculum.

Key Focus Areas:
  • Pre-reading and early literacy skills
  • Basic mathematical concepts
  • Creative arts and expression
  • Social skills and cooperative play
  • Problem-solving and critical thinking

Kindergarten Program (4.5-5.5yrs)

Preparing children for primary school through comprehensive academic and social development.

Key Focus Areas:
  • Reading readiness and phonics
  • Writing and fine motor skills
  • Number sense and early mathematics
  • Science exploration and discovery
  • School readiness and classroom skills

After-School Enrichment (6-10yrs)

Extended learning opportunities in arts, sciences, and technology to complement school curriculum.

Key Focus Areas:
  • STEM activities and projects
  • Creative arts and music
  • Homework support and academic enrichment
  • Physical activities and games
  • Digital literacy and technology skills
